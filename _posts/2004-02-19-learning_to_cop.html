--- layout: post title: Learning to cope with cowardice date: 2004-02-19 00:00:00 category: personal ---

I went to see Mark Thomas play at the Komedia last night, along with Dr Dan, Ju, Ken, NewTom, Steve and DavePh. He was absolutely ace; most of the gig consisted of stories from various protests like DSEI in London ("if someone phones in and says there's a bomb in the building, is that a hoax?") and Menwith Hill), with a screed against Coca Cola taking up much of the second half.

I'd never seen him play live before, but will definitely make an effort to do so again. Well worth it, even if I did walk away with a slight feeling of shame that I don't think and do more about this sort of stuff...
