Well Code Camp is wrapping up, so not too much to report. Last night they threw us a bit of a party. More drinks and food than most people knew what to do with, a BBC DJ, and a couple of slight of hand magicians that were really good; bent spoons, and 50 pound notes popping out of thin air. The folks at Orange have really put quite a bit of effort in organising this event. Its made for quite an enjoyable time.

This morning I was up at 8:00 to see a presentation on Microsoft .Net for mobile devices. Its quite a slick system they have built with .Net, the same classes for windows work on mobiles. Its sort of a Java like model. Not suitable for everything by for pocket pc development its probably the way to go.

Well thats pretty much it for Code Camp. Just the closing ceremonies to come.
