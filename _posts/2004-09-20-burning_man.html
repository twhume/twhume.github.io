Dr Dan came round last night with his video diary of Burning Man, and we sat there gaping at the sights for about 90 minutes whilst he narrated us through the whole journey. That, combined with the galleries that Jay and Dave have put online of all their photos, have persuaded me that next year will be The Year.

It looks like having a lot of the spirit Glastonbury old-timers tell me that festival had and lost, years before I started going...
