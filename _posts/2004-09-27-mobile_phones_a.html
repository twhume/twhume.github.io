From Mobile Pipeline:

"In Britain, the report says, police have confiscated mobile phones from people demonstrating against the war in Iraq."

Or to be more accurate, the police are routinely using anti-terrorist legislation in all sorts of ways: like preventing someone from taking a photo on Brighton beach this week. Presumably anyone who knows what Brighton beach looks like should keep schtum, in the name of national security...
