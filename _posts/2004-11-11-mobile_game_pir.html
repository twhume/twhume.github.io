--- layout: post title: Mobile game piracy might be good, long-term? date: 2004-11-11 00:00:00 ---

From Russell Beattie's notebook, on "N-Gaging Piracy":

"Like you say, maybe piracy is a good thing for the N-gage, if it gets more people playing mobile games and starting to think about phones as a proper gaming platform. It's not good for games developers right now, who are losing revenue (and many of whom will go out of business).

I wonder how accepted MP3 and the like would be if they'd had DRM from day 1."
