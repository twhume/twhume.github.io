Getting the budget out of a client: "When they tell you they don’t have a number say, “Oh, ok. So a $100,000 solution would work for you?” They’ll quickly come back… “Oh no, probably something more around $30K.” BINGO: That’s the budget."

"Guess the budget" is my favourite game in the world, ever.
