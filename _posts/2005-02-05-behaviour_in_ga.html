--- layout: post title: Behaviour in gamers and non-gamers date: 2005-02-05 00:00:00 category: play ---

Greg Costikyan on The Ludic Viewpoint, differences in behaviour between gamers and non-gamers: "...non-gamers tend to approach a new situation asking "what do I need to know?", while gamers tend to ask "How do I game this system to get what I want?""
