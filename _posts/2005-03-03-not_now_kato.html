Self-defense: defense against oneself: "He asked, what were the chances we would be attacked by a trained karate person? Pretty slim to none. So, he continued, what were the biggest threats to our life, property, and liberty? I answered all three questions the same way. The biggest threat to my life, liberty, and property was my own stupidity."
