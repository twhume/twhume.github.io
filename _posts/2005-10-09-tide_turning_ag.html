Tide Turning Against Copy Protection?: "The ball is rolling, and people are starting to realize that yes, DRM and copy protection are pointless and stupid. The argument that they're necessary evils to persuade content providers to make content available digitally is hogwash; they're used as tools to lock people in to formats, products, brands and stores. "
