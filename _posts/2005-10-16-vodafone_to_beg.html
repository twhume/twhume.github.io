Via Keitai-L, Vodafone to begin fixed-rate service for sweethearts: "Vodafone KK said Tuesday it will begin a service on Nov 1 that will allow a subscriber to converse and exchange email with a designated person limitlessly for a fixed monthly fee of 315 yen."
