--- layout: post title: Eden Project team plans eco-rainforest in Lancashire date: 2005-11-21 00:00:00 ---

The guys behind the Eden Project are keeping busy: "Unlike Eden, which uses fossil fuels to heat its domes in winter, Kew of the North is expected to get its fuel for free and would run at a profit even without visitors. Civil engineers working with Grimshaw estimate that it could earn about £12m a year by generating heat and electricity on site and from taking the green waste."
