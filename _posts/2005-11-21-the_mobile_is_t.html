--- layout: post title: The Mobile is the PC of the Developing World date: 2005-11-21 00:00:00 categories: digital-media mobile ---

In the developing world, " the mobile phone combines the functionality of the telephone and the computer, for many people. This is just one of the driving factors that will see the mobile replace the computer as the primary device that most people use most of the time to access the mobile internet - including us in the West, by the way."
