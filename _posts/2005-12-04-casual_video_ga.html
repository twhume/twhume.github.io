'Casual' video games are serious business: "The developers are gamers themselves and always want to push the envelope on the games they make," Olhava adds. "But if they try to make something bigger or more complex -- and people don't play it or buy it -- at the end of the day, business will win out. The developers will get the hint quickly enough that this isn't what the casual gamer wants."
