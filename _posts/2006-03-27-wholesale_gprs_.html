Great news, even if it is only on a single network for now: "Vodafone customers downloading tracks from the MoS or V2’s WAP sites will not incur any data charges on their mobile phone bill beyond the list price of the song."

Helping customers navigate through the impenetrable forest that is mobile data pricing can only be a good thing.
