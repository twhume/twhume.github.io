--- layout: post title: Talent Circle Super Shorts International Film Festival 2006 date: 2006-04-04 00:00:00 categories: digital-media mobile ---

Talent Circle Super Shorts International Film Festival 2006: "The Mobile Film Competition is a competition to find great films suitable for phones and handheld devices. Films must be a minimum of 20 seconds and last no longer than 60 seconds in duration. Entries are welcomed from filmmaking talent in the UK. The best and most innovative films will be screened as part of the Super Shorts Film Festival in July, with the ten best films short-listed to win the award for Best Mobile Film, which will be presented at the awards ceremony in central London."
