Yay, Swedish Beers reasserts itself my favourite mobile networking event... principally because it's a group of nice interesting folk getting hammered in a half-decent bar, and doesn't suffer too much from the press-release-reciting puffery that some other events tend towards.

Had a great time at Nordic Bar last night, mob-handed with Devi and Ribot of FP (if you can call 3 a mob). It was great to catch up with a few folks I'd not seen for a while - Helen, Mike, Nick, Steve and Simon - and meet a few new faces (Oleg and the mighty Masabists).

Thanks to the obsessive photography of Mr Ribot, the evening is well documented. I'm assured that footage will follow.

And big wooo to Helen for organising it all!
