DoCoMo In The “Lifestyle Infrastructure” Business: "Nakamura says the company is in the lifestyle infrastructure business — not selling connectivity, but developing services that allow mobile technology to pervade every aspect of its users’ lives. The focus isn’t so much on coming up with new mobile-specific applications and services, but rather ones that simplify and build on everyday life and experiences."
