Extending party games to mobile: "Perhaps the most interesting aspect of this is that I could imagine participating in a game with my group of close friends. Each round is a quick “I’m thinking of you, and about who you are” sort of casual interaction. I’d want maybe 3 or 4 rounds a day, not too much."

Lurking in my personal LazyWeb is a game where you physically pass your phone around to play... for no reason other than I can imagine it being quite neat, chucking the game from person to person as it clicks and whirrs...
