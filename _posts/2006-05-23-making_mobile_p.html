Making mobile payments work: "There are several issues that need to be addressed to make mobile payments work in practice. In short, if the user can not rely on the phone to make a payment, there has to be a large incentive to risk having to try the transaction twice: once with phone, once with the physical wallet. Ideally, of course, the wallet is unnecessary but that will take a while."
