KPN WAPs i-mode: "In an odd move KPN has launched a new portal, Mobiel startsite, to make i-mode content accessible for non i-mode customers. KPN subscribers or prepaid users with a WAP enable handset, can access up to fifty i-mode sites and news and "538 ringtones"."
