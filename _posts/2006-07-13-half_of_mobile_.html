Half of mobile data virgins say never again | The Registr: "44 per cent of first-timers told NOP they have no intention of using mobile data services again. 29 per cent of data users said they were trying the services out for the first time, with text alerts, video clips and picture messages the most popular services. Almost half cited cost concerns, and one in five cited ease of use issues."
