Social Call: "Last year Future Platforms built a mobile Java-based Sudoku game for Puzzler Media that allowed users to register their scores in a league. "We thought we'd get maybe 5% of people reporting their score," says founder Tom Hume. In fact, a massive 40% of players entered their scores."
