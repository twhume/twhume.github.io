More nepotism... if you're in Brighton and fancy an evening basking in musical delights this Friday, check out Circulus (and the amazing support act preceding them, cough Victoria Hume cough)

"Bad Shepherd" and "Swallows Tail" are particular favourites but there's a huge pile of other noises he's made there, brought to you by the letters M and P and the number 3...
