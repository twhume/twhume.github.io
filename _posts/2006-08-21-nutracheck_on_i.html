--- layout: post title: Nutracheck on I-mode date: 2006-08-21 00:00:00 category: mobile ---

Nutracheck on I-mode: "In the UK, i-mode and Nutracheck have got together to offer a full-on diet programme. You set your weight loss aims, and then enter what you eat on your i-mode phone. You can even enter the barcode number of certain items, such as coffee shop products and alcoholic drinks."
