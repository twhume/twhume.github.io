Fitts Law and softkey optimization: "On a scroll-and-select device, distance is measured by the number of clicks to accomplish the task. Clicks include scrolling through the screen, pushing a Select button, pushing a softkey, scrolling through a softkey menu, and activating an item in a softkey menu.

Distances are increased when the hardware buttons are far from the user’s current thumb position."
