I was quite chuffed to see that the .mobi site which we built for Three, for their 3GSM awards entries, works nicely in the new .mobi emulator... which Carlo has given a bit of a panning to. A hearty slap on the back to Mr Skinner and Mr Ribot at FP for their work on the site.

I'd share his scepticism about the value of emulators - we did real testing on a huge range of devices to get www.three.mobi working as well as it does (after all, we were briefed by our client that it should work on any handset which a 3GSM attendee might use). That said, I guess some tools are better than no tools...
