Making the Most of a Working Day: "Big projects in particular come down to planning and not just planning in the sense that you stick some deadlines in a calendar and leave people to it. It's up to each and every one of the members in a team to assess their to-do lists and decide which of the three modes they need to engage in order to accomplish the tasks and communicate them openly"

I liked the recognition of different "modes of working" in this post too:

  1. If your work requires long periods of un-interrupted concentration
  2. If your work requires short bursts of intensive activity
  3. If Your Work Requires Creative Team Input
