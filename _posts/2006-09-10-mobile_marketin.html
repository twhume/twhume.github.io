My old boss Richard Davies, gets a quote in this Campaign article on mobile messaging: "The bigger agencies are definitely ignoring the potential of mobile. Traditionally, these agencies are always slow to pick up on new technology, and when they do it takes them a while to skill up..."

He's right. Organisations which are optimised to work well in one medium - whether it be print, TV, or web - aren't best positioned to do new media well.
