Are you a Sussex Flash developer?: "Future Platforms are running "Flash on the Move" which gives all local Flash designers the chance to show off their talent by designing a Flash Lite business card for a mobile phone. Designers will need to use Flash Lite using a canvas size of 176x220 pixels and a maximum file size of 150k."
