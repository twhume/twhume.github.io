--- layout: post title: Mind Candy raises funding date: 2006-10-26 00:00:00 categories: business play ---

Mind Candy raises funding: "Today Mind Candy, the UK based developers behind the ARG (Alternative Reality Game) Perplex City, announced they had raised $7 million from Accel Partners and existing investor Index Ventures and NewMedia Spark to fund a major expansion of new products to be announced in early 2007 including the development of its second major puzzle brand aimed at a younger demographic."

Congratulations guys!
