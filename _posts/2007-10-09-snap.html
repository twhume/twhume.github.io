--- layout: post title: SNAP date: 2007-10-09 00:00:00 categories: mobile play software-development --- Interesting titbit from a talk by Anton Gauffin of GameLion: SNAP games can be ported to handsets from non-Nokia manufacturers. Update: just asked, and yep, it's a standard that's going to be kept across handsets from different manufacturers.
