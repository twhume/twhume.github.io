If you're in Brighton and interested in this sort of thing, Joh and I will be running a dress rehearsal for the workshop we've running at Agile2009 in Chicago this August. It'll be held at The Mighty Skiff on Monday 13th July, 7:30pm onwards. The theme for the workshop is exploration of constraints within an iterative design process. That's a posh way of saying that we're going to get you designing stuff for phones in 90 minutes, and look at how how form factors affect what you make.

Expect measured quantities of chaos: we've not run this format before, and one reason for doing a dress rehearsal is to iron out the kinks and, guess what: note what works, what doesn't, and what we don't yet understand.
