Mobile 2.0: Regine Debatty on Mobile & Culture & Arts

Wants to cover how artists, designers and hackers use technology today. Ex-Latin and Greek teacher.

She shows off a series of art projects. I sit in my chair grinning ear to ear:

  • Limor Fried, Wave Bubble, a portable piece of kit to jam nearby mobile signals.
  • the portable cell phone booth.
  • the privacy scarf
  • Bikes Against Bush
  • SMS Guerilla Projector, received praise form the Street Promotions guy at Warner Bros.
  • Parasite, "an independant projection-system that can be attached to subways and other trains with suction pads"
