I've been meaning to post this for a little while; if you're heading to LIFT10 in Geneva this May, you'll have a chance to attend the "Mobile Mountains" Agile/UX workshop which Joh and I have been running over the last 9 months. I'm particularly chuffed about this because LIFT is (as I've mentioned before) one of my favourite events, and some client work prevented my attending in 2009.
